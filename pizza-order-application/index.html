Iman's Pizzeria is an Italian family-owned-and-operated business since the late 1980s. With humble beginnings, Iman's Pizzeria climbed its way up, one pizza at a time.
We believe each customer has unique and special requests and that is why we specialize in custom-made pizzas. Our focus is on our customers, making sure we're catering to each individual. Whether it's your first time trying Iman's Pizzeria or you're a loyal customer of ours, we warmly welcome you.
